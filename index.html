Story

Progressive music needs progressive talent, honing his craft for more than decade, Lucid takes drum and music head on, with a strong grasp of the music and its forward thinking ideals, avant-garde lyrics, and a mature hosting style.

He has hosted sets for the likes of Klute, Goldie, Peshay, Zinc, Saburuko, D-Bridge, ASC, Noisia, Instra:mental, Seba, Paradox, Blu Marten, Pascal, Hazard, The Mixologists, Concord Dawn, Dylan, Noisia, Chris Inperspective, Pieter K, Black Sun Empire, Blame, Pendulum, Capital J, Zinc, Marcus Visionary, Stranjah, Gremlinz, Mutt, NC-17, 0=0, Mystical Influence, Dx, Makoto and many others.

His vocals can be found on Pronobozo’s Hackwave (2010) and Zero = One = Everything (2007), KnifeHandChop’s “How I left You” album on tigerbeat6.
